## Engagement Strategy

### Who to Reply To
1. **SMB owners** in target industries (auto, beauty, medical)
2. **Safety consultants** (potential partners)
3. **AI/No-code builders** (peer network)
4. **Micro-SaaS founders** (similar journey)
5. **Industry influencers** (expand reach)

### How to Reply
- Add value, don't just say "great post"
- Share relevant experience or insight
- Ask thoughtful follow-up questions
- Be concise (1-2 sentences max)

### Targets to Engage Daily
- 5 accounts with 1K-10K followers (responsive)
- 3 accounts with 10K-50K followers (reach)
- 2 accounts with 50K+ followers (visibility lottery)
