## 4. LLM/AI Discovery Strategy

### How to Get Cited by ChatGPT/Perplexity/Claude

1. **Be the definitive answer** — Write clear, specific answers to common questions
2. **Use structured data** — Schema markup for SoftwareApplication, FAQPage
3. **Target "People Also Ask"** — Answer questions in concise paragraphs (40-60 words)
4. **Get mentioned elsewhere** — Guest posts, partnerships, citations train LLMs
5. **Create original data** — Surveys, statistics, research gets cited

### AI Search Optimization Checklist
- [ ] Add FAQ schema to key pages
- [ ] Write 40-60 word answers to common questions
- [ ] Create comparison tables (LLMs love structured data)
- [ ] Include specific numbers and statistics
- [ ] Use clear headings that match search queries
